How to Play

Game Overview

Welcome to Littagrams! Test your book knowledge by identifying classic books from their characters.

How It Works

  • You start with 100 points, and the name of a minor character from a classic book. If you can guess the book, congratulations! You win 100 points
  • If you can’t, click "Reveal Next Character" to gradually unveil a slightly more prominent character from the book, although you’ll lose 10 points for every character you reveal.

Scoring System

  • You start with 100 points for each book
  • Each character reveal costs 10 points
  • Each incorrect guess costs 10 points
  • Skipping a book results in 0 points

Tips

  • The bigger a character's circle, the more often that character is mentioned
  • The thickness of the line between characters shows how often they appear together in the novel.
  • Don't worry about exact spelling, but try to get the title mostly correct
